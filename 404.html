Strait is the gate—Nothing found (error 404)

Regrettably, there is nothing at this address, apart from this, though this is only here as total silence would be unbearable.

Once upon a midnight dreary, while I websurfed, weak and weary,
Over many a strange and spurious website of hot chicks galore,
While I clicked my fav’rite bookmark, suddenly there came a warning,
And my heart was filled with mourning, mourning for my dear amour.
“’Tis not possible,” I muttered, “give me back my cheap hardcore!”—Quoth the server, “404”.
A lovely poem from Plinko
